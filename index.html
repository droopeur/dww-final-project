If you are a passionate musician, Art Collab is for you. Reinvent your museum exerience : dare mixing music with painting and build a new network in your field.

Get Started

How to use ArtCollab ?

Let's get started

Find the perfect match

When you are facing a masterpiece in a museum you just have to take a picture of it in the app. Then you have the choice between five different musics that ArtCollab considers as suitable for the masterpiece you snaped : vote for the best one ! If none of the five is perfectly appropriated, feel free to add to your own.

Share with your community

Share contents freely on your personal page in order to promote your musical and visual association or to showcase your new proposal. You can also discover other users creations on the ArtCollab network.

Get informed of other users choices and exchange with them

You can have access to what users chose between the five musics suggested by the app. Discover if you followed the crowd or not at all ? Then let’s launch debates between users and meet new friends through the ArtCollab tchat.
